Funeral of Stress

This work is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
I wrote this piece during my senior year of high school, in a time where I was having trouble coping with the stress. I had myself taken off my medication, but I increasingly had issues coping with everything going on around me, effectively regressing to how I was before I was put on it. This piece came from how I felt in that stage of my life.
